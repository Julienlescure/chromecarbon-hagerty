04 · Challenges
Where We Need to Evolve
01
Partner Model Change — OneTreePlanted
Our main reforestation partner has moved away from a $1-per-tree model to a bulk donation structure requiring a $25K upfront commitment. This forces us to rethink how we price and communicate donations to enthusiasts — an opportunity, not just a constraint.
02
Not a Programme for Everyone
The ECO programme has generated enthusiasm among a loyal core, but has also attracted limited and isolated criticism from some customers who are sceptical of carbon offsetting as a concept. We need to speak more precisely to our true audience.
03
“Carbon Offset” — A Brand Problem
The programme name carries significant baggage. Despite focusing exclusively on reforestation, the “offset” label damages credibility with exactly the informed enthusiasts we most want to attract.
5 / 13
05 · Rebrand
A Bolder Name for
a Bolder Programme

● Drop “Carbon Offset”

Replace with language around reforestation. More accurate, more credible, harder to criticise. The programme has always been about funding reforestation — the name should say so.

● Lead with the Scenery We Drive Through

Car enthusiasts care about the landscapes they drive through. The winding roads, the mountain passes, the forests glimpsed through a windscreen. Reforestation protects those landscapes even if the trees are planted far from the road. We drive through nature, and we owe something back to it.

● Align with Hagerty’s Mission

“Saving Driving” is Hagerty’s brand platform. The programme name should connect directly to that narrative — protecting the beautiful environments that make driving worth doing.

● New Model Needs New Framing

Moving from per-tree to per-tonne donations requires a narrative reset anyway — the rebrand is the natural moment to do it cleanly and with conviction.

6 / 13
06 · New Donation Model
From Per-Tree to Per-Tonne

The Calculator Stays Front and Centre

Members still enter mileage and MPG. The calculator computes CO² in lbs and converts it to a donation using a transparent $/tonne rate. The emotional hook — “your drive generated X lbs of CO²” — remains. Only the output changes: from a tree count to a dollar contribution.

Why $/Tonne is More Future-Proof

  • Compatible with any reforestation partner globally — not just those selling trees by the unit
  • Aligns with how the voluntary carbon market prices forest restoration ($25–$65/tonne)
  • Easier to update the rate when switching or adding partners
  • More credible with informed audiences — the tonne is the recognised scientific unit

Donation Handling — Fee-Free for Members

100% of each donation is passed to the reforestation partner (net of card processing fees). Chrome Carbon propose to limit its handling overhead to $5 per contribution and add it to its donations & Certification fees. A cleaner more transparent model for the enthusiast and a minimal additional charge for Hagerty.

07 · Referral Programme
Growing the Community
from Within

A New Recruitment Strategy

Rather than relying on broad Hagerty marketing campaigns, the referral programme turns our most engaged members into ambassadors — reaching fellow enthusiasts who share the same values, not just anyone in the Hagerty database.

How It Works

  • Members invite friends via a personal referral link
  • When a referred friend joins and makes their first contribution, the member adds a friend to its “Fellow enthusiasts” count and progress in the dedicated leaderboard that ranks members by successful referrals
  • An additional incentive of $5 top-up on their next contribution for each referred friend who joins could also be a cost-effective way to acquire new members

Beyond Hagerty’s Existing Pool

Referrals are not restricted to existing Hagerty clients. Members can invite any car enthusiast — expanding the programme’s reach organically beyond Hagerty’s current customer base.

08 · Dual Leaderboard
Friendly Competition
that Drives Action

Two Ways to Compete

  • Miles Leaderboard — ranked by total miles acted on. Rewards consistent, long-term contributors.
  • Fellow Enthusiasts Leaderboard — ranked by friends successfully referred. Rewards community building and word-of-mouth growth.

Why a Dual Leaderboard Works

  • Not every member drives thousands of miles — the referral board gives everyone a way to compete
  • Creates two distinct incentive loops: drive more, and recruit more
  • Public visibility of rankings adds social accountability and pride
  • Transforms a one-off donation into an ongoing engagement mechanic

Community Building Effect

Members return regularly to check their rank, log new miles, and see how referred friends are contributing — building a habit rather than a one-off payment.

09 · Member Personal Space
A Logged-In Home for
Every Enthusiast

Personal Dashboard

  • At-a-glance summary: total trees, miles logged, CO² acted on
  • Current rank on both leaderboards
  • Referral status: invitations sent, friends joined, bonuses earned

Virtual Garage

  • Save vehicles with make, model, year & MPG
  • Saved vehicles pre-fill the calculator automatically — no re-entry needed
  • Supports multiple vehicles per member for accurate per-car tracking

Contribution History

  • Full log of past contributions: date, miles, CO² and donation amount
  • Easy one-click access to log a new trip or yearly mileage
  • Makes impact visible over time, reinforcing long-term commitment

10 · Outcomes
The Programme We’re Building Towards

A Self-Reinforcing Community

Referrals, leaderboards and a personal space create a flywheel: engaged members recruit new members, who become engaged themselves. Growth without relying solely on Hagerty campaigns.

Higher Retention

Habit-forming mechanics — the garage, leaderboard rank, contribution history — give members reasons to return and contribute regularly, not just once at sign-up.

A Credible, Trusted Programme

A cleaner brand name, per-tonne pricing, and transparent partner relationships make the programme harder to criticise and easier to be proud of.

Partner Flexibility

The $/tonne model opens the door to NFF, Tree Canada, Woodland Trust and others — local, credible, government-affiliated partners with transparent reporting.

Reach Beyond Hagerty’s Base

Referrals can bring in enthusiasts who are not Hagerty clients. The programme becomes a standalone brand asset — a community of drivers who care.

Impact Hagerty Can Prove

The existing reporting ledger stays. Every contribution is tracked, every project funded recorded. Precise, auditable impact numbers for stakeholders, press and members.
